# Quant Platform Radar Source Policy

Use only public content from JoinQuant, Guorn, ThinkTrader, public search snippets, and public metadata.

Do not:

- bypass login walls, captchas, paywalls, robots restrictions, private APIs, or rate limits;
- republish long article bodies, strategy code, or proprietary data;
- treat platform marketing pages as market evidence without separate confirmation;
- treat backtest titles or community snippets as investment advice.

Evidence grading:

- Strong: official public product/data updates, exchange filings, company announcements, or credible financial media that confirm the platform clue.
- Medium: public platform pages, public community titles with enough context, repeated snippets across sources.
- Weak: single search snippet, inaccessible page title, social repost, or old tutorial content.
- Needs checking: a useful but incomplete clue that needs source access, live market data, or manual verification.

When mapping to stocks, separate:

- `quant style`: factor, timing, rotation, ETF, high dividend, microcap, momentum, reversal, intraday, CTA, options, or portfolio construction;
- `industry theme`: AI, semiconductor, PCB, CPO, robot, power grid, energy storage, medicine, consumer, financials, commodities;
- `data/tool clue`: new data source, API, backtest feature, live trading integration, or platform signal.
