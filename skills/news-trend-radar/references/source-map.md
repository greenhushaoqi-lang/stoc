## Domestic China Sources

### NewsNow

Main site: `https://newsnow.busiyi.world/`

Use for: domestic Chinese real-time news, hot social topics, platform-wide trend discovery, policy/news pulse. The public project describes NewsNow as a real-time news system with source integration and default caching; treat it as a discovery and clustering source, not final proof.

When using:

1. Search or browse the relevant channel/topic.
2. Capture headline, source, time, and repeated mentions.
3. Cross-check high-impact items with original media, official announcements, exchange filings, or ministry/company sources.

### 今日热榜 / TopHub

Main site: `https://tophub.today/`

Technology channel: `https://tophub.today/c/tech`

Use for: Chinese internet heat, tech/product topics, platform sentiment, consumer attention, hot search diffusion.

When using:

1. Start with the relevant category such as tech, finance, news, or platform hot lists.
2. Record rank, source platform, headline, and whether the topic persists across lists.
3. Treat high heat as sentiment signal; confirm real business impact elsewhere.

## Scoring Rubric

Score each narrative from 0-5:

- **Freshness**: 0 old, 5 breaking or rapidly updating.
- **Source quality**: 0 social-only, 5 primary/credible repeated reporting.
- **Cross-source confirmation**: 0 single item, 5 multiple independent sources.
- **Market linkage**: 0 vague, 5 clear ticker/sector/commodity/policy mapping.
- **Novelty**: 0 consensus, 5 new or underpriced narrative.

Priority:

- 20-25: urgent radar item.
- 15-19: watch closely.
- 10-14: useful context.
- below 10: likely noise unless user specifically asks.

## Output Fields

For every important item include:

- headline/topic;
- source URLs;
- timestamp or approximate freshness;
- why it matters;
- affected assets, stocks, sectors, or themes;
- evidence grade;
- next verification source.
